Welcome!

This site is called "My Video Game Archive". The goal behind the site is to give people an ability to look for games that they enjoyed back in the day, or allow people to discover games they did not know existed. This also allows users like you to suggest games you want to have added to the database. Then you can be an active participant in the site. That is where the "My" comes in. We host it, and it becomes part of your virtual library.
